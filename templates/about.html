üîê What is MPIN Analyzer?

MPIN Analyzer is a security evaluation tool designed to check the strength of your Mobile PIN (MPIN). It helps identify weak patterns such as:

  • Commonly used or guessable PINs (like 1234, 0000, 111111)
  • Sequential patterns (e.g., 123456 or 4321)
  • Personal demographic matches (e.g., birthdays, anniversaries)

By analyzing these aspects, the app gives a score and strength label (e.g., WEAK or STRONG) and provides suggestions to improve your PIN security.
